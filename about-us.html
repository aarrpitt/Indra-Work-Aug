Hi, We're Team Indra!

Who are we?

An innovative collective of like-minded folks providing sustainable system "To make good quality water accessible and affordable to industry and society"

What we do?

At Indra we develop a sustainable system with powerful automated analytics to build cheaper, smaller and energy efficient systems for treating waste water and make it accessible globally.

What we have done?

Indra has developed de-centralized electrically driven water management system or society & industry to offset freshwater demand. At present Indra has provided more than 300+ million litres of treated waste water to the society and still its counting.
