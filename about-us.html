Who are we, what we do, what we have done

Decentralization is the key to solving the water problem. We are Decentralized.
Indra's secret mission is no secret at all
Build an affordable electrically driven water treatment system for society & industry to offset freshwater demand • Use the money to build an affordable drinking water system • While doing above, provide sustainable and efficient solutions for air cleaning, waste management, energy recovery & water treatment to build a circular economy of waste, water and energy Indra's flow technology is the best in class in broad spectrum pollution removal.
We won the 'Urban Water Challenge' at World Water Week 2019' in Stockholm among 226 companies across 38 countries.
Indra goes beyond water purification We are one of the few fully integrated water companies driving the world's inevitable shift towards a data driven sustainable future Indra is not done solving problems. We're here to stay. We are ahead of competition. We are building superior solutions for tomorrow without setting ourselves any limits.
