Hi, We're Team Indra!

Who are we?

Solving the toughest waste water management through turnkey innovative technology and smart analytics platform which provides access to good quality water to all by sustainable water and energy management.

What we do?

At Indra we develop a sustainable system with powerful automated analytics to build cheaper, smaller and energy efficient systems for treating waste water and make it accessible globally.
